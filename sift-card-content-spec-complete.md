# Sift — Card Content Spec (v3 — Clothing Focused)

Voice: The friend who asks one good question and then shuts up.

---

## Card Order in Deck

1. **First card** (always on top, instructional)
2. **Pillar cards** (2 per active pillar, randomly selected, shuffled together)
3. **Last card** (always at the bottom, closing)

---

## First Card

**Headline:**
Your cart's not going anywhere.

**Body:**
Shuffle through, take your time, and jot down some thoughts.

|--------|------|

---

## Deck Logic

| User selected | Cards in deck |
|---------------|---------------|
| 1 pillar | First card + 2 pillar cards + last card = **4 cards** |
| 2 pillars | First card + 4 pillar cards + last card = **6 cards** |
| 3 pillars | First card + 6 pillar cards + last card = **8 cards** |

Pillar cards are randomly selected (2 from each active pillar's pool of 4) and shuffled together so pillars alternate rather than appearing in blocks. First card is always first. Last card is always last.
